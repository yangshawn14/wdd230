Membership Benefits

  • NP Membership (Non-Profit)

    Price: Free

    This level is tailored for non-profit organizations and community groups. Enjoy access to our networking events, volunteer opportunities, and promotional support for your cause.

  • Bronze Membership

    Price: $50/month

    Bronze members receive all the benefits of our NP Membership plus exclusive access to member-only webinars and workshops. Showcase your organization on our platform and gain visibility within the community.

  • Silver Membership

    Price: $100/month

    Silver membership includes all the benefits of Bronze Membership with added perks such as discounted advertising rates and priority listing in our directory. Connect with industry leaders and expand your network through our specialized events.

  • Gold Membership

    Price: $200/month

    Gold members enjoy top-tier benefits including premium advertising placement, VIP access to all events, and personalized support from our team. Take advantage of our concierge services to elevate your organization's visibility and impact within the community.
